# Harvesting Tools — MANAGER Training Video

**Audience:** Regional managers. Their tools live on the **manager dashboard** (regional-manager page), not the rep map. Focus = oversee the team live, plan their day, assign territory, and track results.
**Film on:** the manager dashboard with office preview — `…/regional-manager/<manager-token>?preview=1` (preview unlocks the Harvesting tools without the certification). For the rep-side sandbox, use `…/?mode=harvest&demo=1`.
**Note:** Managers must pass the **manager tool-training certification** to unlock these in real life — call that out in the video.
**Length:** ~6 min · **[SCREEN]** = what to record · **[SAY]** = paste into HeyGen

---

## Setup (do this once)
- **Film on the manager dashboard** with office preview: `…/regional-manager/<manager-token>?preview=1` — preview unlocks the Harvesting tools without the certification. For any rep-side screen, use the sandbox `…/?mode=harvest&demo=1`.
- ⚠️ **Live-data warning:** the Live Team Map and leaderboard show a *real* zone's reps and numbers. Film when nothing sensitive is on screen, or blur rep names in editing.
- **Record on the device you present from** — desktop or tablet is fine for the manager tools (more screen room than a phone).
- Capture each scene as its own short clip. The **[SCREEN]** line in each scene is your shot list.

---

## SCENE 1 — Welcome (0:00–0:25)
**[SCREEN]** Manager dashboard open, scrolled to the Harvesting tools section.
**[SAY]** "Welcome. As a regional manager, the Harvesting tools give you a live window into your whole team in the field — where everyone is, what they're doing, and what they're producing — plus the controls to plan their day and set their territory. Let's walk through them."

## SCENE 2 — The Live Team Map (0:25–1:45) ⭐
**[SCREEN]** The **Live Team Map** for the manager's zone: rep dots with names, breadcrumb trails, today's counts. Tap a rep to highlight their trail + last action.
**[SAY]** "This is your Live Team Map — just your zone's reps. Each dot is a rep, right where they are, updated every thirty seconds. The line behind them is their trail from the last three hours, so you can see the route they've actually walked. Tap a rep to see their last action and today's numbers. If someone's been sitting still for fifteen minutes — or closed their map — they drop to idle, so at a glance you know who's working and who's parked. No calling around for updates; it's all right here."

## SCENE 3 — Enhanced Planned Day (1:45–3:00) ⭐
**[SCREEN]** The **Enhanced Planned Day** panel. Trigger the split; show the zone's leads dividing into balanced clusters, one color per senior rep.
**[SAY]** "Enhanced Planned Day is how you hand every senior a ready-made day. Tap to plan, and the map takes all the fresh IQ leads and no-sits in your zone and splits them into balanced sections — one per senior rep, including yourself — each a tight, drive-efficient block so nobody's crossing the zone or knocking a teammate's door. Once you publish it, each senior opens their map to a purple banner — 'your day is planned by your manager' — and taps 'Start my day.' You set the plan once; the whole team runs it."

## SCENE 4 — Draw a territory section (3:00–3:50)
**[SCREEN]** Select a rep, then **drag a box** on the map to draw their section (BoxDrawer). Show the rectangle tracking the drag.
**[SAY]** "Need to override and hand a specific rep a specific area? Pick the rep, then just drag a box on the map to draw their territory. That becomes their section for the day. Great for putting your closer on the hot street, or steering a new rep to an easy block. Draw it, and it's theirs."

## SCENE 5 — Assign appointments (3:50–4:40)
**[SCREEN]** The **Assign Appointments** split map: RED pins = senior sales reps, appointment addresses to hand out. Assign one to a rep.
**[SAY]** "When appointments come in that need a body, the Assign Appointments view puts them on a map next to your senior sales reps — reps in red. You can see who's closest and hand each appointment to the right rep in one tap. It lands on their day and in JobNimbus, assigned to them. No guesswork about who covers what."

## SCENE 6 — The leaderboard & numbers (4:40–5:35)
**[SCREEN]** The **Team Standings** leaderboard with the three buckets: **Inspections signed · Sales · Harvest (appointments booked from the map)**. Expand the Harvest bucket to show per-rep booked counts. Glance at the Appointments→Sales conversion report with its ⚠ fix flags.
**[SAY]** "Results live on your leaderboard, in three buckets: inspections signed, sales closed, and harvest — appointments your reps booked straight off the map. Open the harvest bucket to see exactly who's booking and who's not. And the appointments-to-sales report breaks down each rep's conversion, flagging any deal that needs cleaning up in JobNimbus with a warning — so you always know what to coach and what to fix."

## SCENE 7 — Wrap (5:35–6:00)
**[SCREEN]** Zoom out on the live map with the team spread across the zone.
**[SAY]** "That's your toolkit: watch the team live, plan their day in one tap, set territory where you need it, and track every result. One more thing — you unlock these by passing the manager tool training, same as your reps pass theirs. Do that, and your whole zone runs on one screen. Let's go lead."
