# DoorDispatcher — Video Storyboard / Shot List

Use this alongside the script (`harvest-training-video-script.md`). For each scene it tells you **exactly what to open, what to tap, and what the frame should show**, so capturing the footage is a 10-minute job.

## Setup (do this once)

- **Film in PRACTICE MODE** so nothing is saved and no real rep's data or GPS is touched. Link:
  `https://free-roof-inspections.netlify.app/?mode=harvest&test=sr&demo=1`
  You'll see a purple **"PRACTICE MODE — try anything, nothing you do here is saved"** banner. That's expected.
- **Record on a phone** (screen-record: iPhone = Control Center → Record; Android = Quick Settings → Screen Record). Phone footage matches what reps actually use.
- Before each scene, **zoom the map into a real neighborhood** (South Florida has the most pins) so it looks like a real day, not the whole state.
- Capture each scene as its own short clip. Re-do any take freely — practice mode never saves.

---

### SHOT 1 — Welcome (matches Script Scene 1)
- **Open:** the practice link. Zoom into a neighborhood dense with blue/pink pins.
- **Frame shows:** the map full of pins, "DoorDispatcher" title, your level badge.
- **Action:** just a slow finger-drag/zoom so it feels alive.
- **On-screen text:** *"Your daily door-knocking map"*

### SHOT 2 — What the pins mean (Scene 2)
- **Open:** same view. Tap **Show all** so every color appears.
- **Action:** tap each chip in the top row one at a time — **IQ**, **Appointment**, **No sit**, **Not Interested** — so the map filters to just that color as you name it.
- **Frame shows:** the color changing on the map as each chip toggles.
- **On-screen text:** label each color as it's shown — *"Blue = fresh lead," "Pink = no-sit," "Green = appointment," "Black = not interested"*

### SHOT 3 — Plan your day around an appointment (Scene 3) ⭐ hero shot
- **Setup:** tap **✏️ Add test appt**, then tap a spot on the map (a neighborhood), then set a time a couple hours ahead. (This stands in for a real booked appointment.)
- **Action:** tap **📅 Have an appt? Plan your day!** → set start time → **Build my plan**.
- **Frame shows:** the route drawing — green numbered stops filling in around the appointment pin, with the drive line connecting them. **Zoom out** so the whole planned day is visible (this is the "wow" moment — reps should see it build).
- **Then:** tap the appointment pin, show the **Appt done** button.
- **On-screen text:** *"One tap plans your whole day around your appointment"*

> Note: On a real rep's phone this also appears **automatically** as a banner the moment they open the map ("You have 1 appointment today — first at 11 AM"). If you want to show that, film it separately on a real rep link that has an appointment today — but the practice flow above tells the same story safely.

### SHOT 4 — Route an area, no appointment (Scene 4)
- **Setup:** reload the practice link (clears the plan).
- **Action:** tap **▢ Route an area**, then drag a box around a neighborhood.
- **Frame shows:** the route instantly threading through the doors inside the box.
- **On-screen text:** *"No appointment? Just draw a box — it routes the doors for you"*

### SHOT 5 — Working a door (Scene 5)
- **Action:** tap any pin on your route to open the door card. Tap a status (e.g. **Not home**), then re-open a pin and change it (shows re-statusing).
- **Frame shows:** the door card with the address and the row of status buttons; a pin changing color after you tap.
- **On-screen text:** *"Tap the door, tap the result — saved instantly, shared with your team"*

### SHOT 6 — Book & sign from the door (Scene 6)
- **Action:** on a door card, tap the **Schedule appointment** action and set a time (in practice mode). Then show the **Sign Inspection** button opening the pre-filled claim form.
- **Frame shows:** the appointment being set; the claim form opening already filled with the address.
- **On-screen text:** *"Book it or sign it right at the door — straight into JobNimbus"*

### SHOT 7 — Self-gen: a roof that isn't pinned (Scene 7)
- **Action:** tap the **drop-a-pin** control, place it on an empty house. Show the **"Owner occupied?"** check result, then the **Sign / Retail / Pending** options.
- **Frame shows:** the owner-occupied check result card, then the action buttons.
- **On-screen text:** *"Spot a bad roof? Drop a pin — we check who owns it for you"*

### SHOT 8 — Go-backs (Scene 8)
- **Action:** open the **Today's go-backs** card. Show the dated go-back pins and the inline **Damage / No-Damage / Retail** actions.
- **Frame shows:** the go-backs list with a couple of entries and the action buttons.
- **On-screen text:** *"Second trips stay organized in your go-backs list"*

### SHOT 9 — Wrap up (Scene 9)
- **Action:** zoom out to show a neighborhood with a mix of statused pins and a completed route line.
- **Frame shows:** a "worked day" — colorful, done.
- **On-screen text:** *"Open it, follow it, close. Go get 'em."*
