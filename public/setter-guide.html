📞 Appointment Setter — Quick Guide

Everything you need to book a retail appointment in under a minute.

1. Your link

free-roof-inspections.netlify.app/?mode=setter
📌 Save it once: open the link, then tap your browser's Share → Add to Home Screen (or bookmark it). After that it's a one-tap app — you never have to click a link again.

2. How to book an appointment

  1. 1Pick your name on the first screen (Viviana / Hannah / Dustin).
  2. 2Type the homeowner's address. Google will suggest it as you type — tap the correct match.
  3. 3If they're already in JobNimbus, tap their name. If not, tap "Create new account" and enter first name, last name, mobile, and email.
  4. 4Choose the lead source (IQ is the default — or Facebook, Referral, Yard Sign, etc.).
  5. 5Pick a time. You'll see one week at a time — use Next week → if needed. You do not pick a rep; the system assigns the right one for you.
  6. 6Tap Book. That's it. ✅
The blue ← Back button (top-left) is always there if you need to step back.

3. You do NOT touch JobNimbus 🙌

The portal does everything in JobNimbus for you automatically — it creates the homeowner, creates the job, sets the appointment at the time you picked, assigns the rep, and sets the lead source. If the homeowner was a "No Sit – Need to Reschedule," it even re-sets that existing deal for you. You never have to open or edit anything in JobNimbus.

4. Safety check (for the first couple weeks) 🔎

Just while you're getting used to it, confirm each booking actually landed:

  • After you book, tap 📋 Today (top-right) — your appointment should be listed there.
  • Then open the JobNimbus Calendar and confirm the appointment shows at the time you set.
If it's in both places, it worked perfectly. If something ever looks off, screenshot it and tell Neal — don't try to fix it in JobNimbus.

5. Important: see ALL appointments on the JN calendar 👀

Appointments land on the assigned rep's calendar — so to see them all:

On the JobNimbus Calendar, look at the left side under Team Members and click "Select All" (check everyone). If you only have a few people checked, you won't see most appointments — including the ones you just booked.
