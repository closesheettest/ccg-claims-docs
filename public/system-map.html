Inspector lifecycle
1 · Job reaches the inspector — 3 ways
Admin assigns (Assign Inspections) → inspector_id + claimed_at Inspector self-claims from "Available near you" pool Pool sorted by distance from home/GPS · capped at max_distance_miles no SMS on assign — shows in their app list
2 · Route & photograph
Route optimizer (nearest-neighbor) from home or current GPS · Apple/Google Maps deep links Photo wizard (house # → each story/slope) · resized 1600px · uploaded live to Storage inspection-photos/
3 · Submit a result — 4 outcomes
Damage
result=damage + photos cf_string_34=Damage + photos→Files + Note cert PDF→Documents · go-back task type 25 → PA Ops Hub (photos + signed PDF)
No Damage
result=no_damage cf_string_34=No Damage · cert · task type 23 · no PA
Retail
result=retail cf_string_34=Retail · record_type→Lead · loc→Retail · task type 24 · no PA
Lost (cancelled at the door)
result=lost + lost_reason cf_string_34=Lost + Note · no photos/cert/PA
rep + homeowner result texts/emails built but OFF (AUTO_NOTIFY=false)
4 · Gated inspector? (requires_confirmation)
Result HELD · pending_confirmation=true · nothing fires to JN / cert / PA Manager confirms → full fan-out replays (can change the result) Reject → clears result, job reopens
The hourly push-to-JN cron skips held rows, so a gated result can't slip out.
5 · Re-inspect / retake (wrong house)
Inspector "♻ Re-inspect" on a finished job → wipes photos + result + cert stamp JN Note: "photos cleared, cert will be replaced" · job reopens
6 · Homeowner cancels mid-inspection
